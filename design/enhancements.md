# Planned Enhancements

This document outlines future features and improvements for EyesPie.

## Gameplay & Challenge Variety

### Timed Challenges ⏱
Create urgency and excitement for both clue-makers and guessers.
- Countdown timers for challenges
- Bonus points for quick guesses
- Real-time challenge competitions

### Themed Challenge Packs
Curated gameplay experiences:
- "Street Food Edition" — culinary discoveries
- "Historic Landmarks" — cultural exploration
- "Nature Walks" — outdoor adventures
- "Urban Exploration" — city discoveries

### Chain Challenges
Series of linked clues where each correct guess unlocks the next stage.
- Progressive difficulty
- Story-driven experiences
- Team-based chain completion

## Social & Community

### Friend Groups & Teams
Allow collaborative guessing or competitive team play.
- Create private friend groups
- Team challenges and competitions
- Group leaderboards and stats

### User-Generated Leaderboards
Custom boards for friend circles:
- Personal leaderboards
- Challenge-specific rankings
- Time-based competitions

### Clue Remixing
Let players take an existing challenge and put their own spin on it.
- Fork challenges
- Add custom clues
- Share variations

## Context & Immersion

### Dynamic Difficulty Scaling
AI adjusts hint complexity based on the player's history.
- Adaptive clue generation
- Personalized challenge difficulty
- Skill-based matchmaking

### Weather/Time-Based Challenges
Bonus points for capturing clues in certain real-world conditions.
- Weather API integration
- Time-of-day bonuses
- Seasonal events

### Hidden Local Gems Mode
Spotlight lesser-known spots within a player's radius.
- Location-based discovery
- Community-curated spots
- Exploration rewards

## Motivation & Retention

### Daily Streak Rewards 🔥
Unlock special frames or animations for consistency.
- Consecutive login bonuses
- Streak multipliers
- Exclusive rewards

### Achievement Badges
Quirky, shareable rewards:
- "Blur Master" — expert obfuscation
- "Speed Guesser" — rapid identification
- "Explorer" — location diversity
- "Social Butterfly" — community engagement

### Seasonal Events
Time-limited challenge modes tied to festivals or holidays.
- Holiday-themed content
- Special challenge types
- Limited-time rewards

## Accessibility & Personalization

### Customizable UI Themes
Adapt colors, fonts, and layouts to user preference.
- Color scheme selection
- Font size adjustment
- Layout preferences

### Audio Clues
Record voice hints for a different sensory experience.
- Voice recording
- Audio playback
- Text-to-speech

### Accessibility Features
Making play more accessible for all:
- High-contrast mode
- Large-text mode
- Screen reader support
- Haptic feedback
